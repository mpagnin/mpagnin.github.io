Adventure Tours

Hyalite Canyon

Following our instructors, with the mountain bike you will follow a dirt path along the Hyalite creek.
The long but moderately steep climb into the canyon will end at the Hyalite Reservoir. Here you can enjoy beautiful views of the high peaks of the Gallatin Range.
After resting and refreshing you will be ready for the fun descent that will take you to the valley.

Length

The length of the route is a total of 53.55 miles

Climb

2,091 ft.
Start elevation: 4,700 ft.
Max elevation: 6,791 ft.

Skill Level

Easy to moderate. The tour is not particularly difficult, but we recommend it for people trained to take long dirt ascents.

Cost

$125.00
The price includes a packed lunch with beverage.

Location

Located 9 miles south of Bozeman in the foothills of the Gallatin Range.

West Bridger-Foothills Road Loop

A nice tour on roads with little traffic near the city. Located along the western side of the Bridger Range on the north edge of Bozeman, it offers beautiful views of the Gallatin Valley and the southern end of the Bridgers. After an initial stretch on paved roads, more than half of the route is on a dirt road.

Length

The length of the route is a total of 15.9 miles

Climb

400 ft.
Start elevation: 4,700 ft.
Max elevation: 5,100 ft.

Skill Level

Easy. The tour is also suitable for families with children over 8 years.

Cost

$35.00
The price includes beverage.

Location

Located on the north edge of Bozeman.
